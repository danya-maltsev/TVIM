Kod Absoluta — RU Market

Minimalism that
converts at 39%.

With 88% of traffic arriving on mobile, the design brief was simple: remove every possible point of friction. The result was a scroll-native, single-flow UI with zero distractions — thumb-reachable CTAs, content-first hierarchy, and a visual language soft enough to disarm, precise enough to sell.

A friction-free purchase path and emotionally resonant aesthetic aren't in tension — they're the same thing. When visitors feel safe and seen, they convert. This project proves it at scale.
